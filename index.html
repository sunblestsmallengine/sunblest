Who I am

James Ibey

Sunblest Small Engine Repair is owned and operated by James Ibey, a Hoosier based in Fishers, Indiana. As a father of two college graduates, one from Ball State and the other from Purdue, James understands the value of hard work and dedication. He brings that same energy to every lawnmower, trimmer, or generator he services. With over 30 years of hands-on experience, James has built his reputation on integrity, reliability, and passion for helping people with his skilled technical repair.

James started Sunblest with one simple goal: to make small engine repair convenient and stress-free. As a mobile repair technician, he comes directly to your home, so you don’t have to haul your equipment to a shop. Whether it’s a quick tune-up or a complex fix, he gets it running and gets it done right.

Awarded the 2024 Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite, Sunblest is more than a repair service. It’s a neighbor you can trust. Call today!

Services

I Provide Quality Engine Repair Services

  • Annual Service

    Yearly check up and tailored maintenance package to ensure your engine runs smoothly and reliably all season long.

  • Oil Change

    Drains and replaces old oil to protect your engine and keep it lubricated.

  • Carburetor Cleaning

    Removes buildup from the carburetor to improve fuel efficiency and engine performance.

  • Blade Sharpening

    Restores the cutting edge of your mower blades for a clean, efficient cut.

  • Blade Replacement

    Installs new belts to keep your equipment running without slippage or wear.

  • Tire Repair

    Fixes punctured or flat tires to get your equipment rolling again.

  • Welding

    Provides strong, lasting repairs for broken or cracked metal parts on your equipment.

  • Fuel Line Replacement

    Installs new fuel lines to prevent leaks and ensure consistent fuel delivery.

  • Spark Plug Service

    Repairs or cleans spark plugs to maintain strong ignition and smooth engine starts.

  • Cable Replacement

    Replaces throttle, brake, or drive cables to restore control and functionality.

  • Electrical Repairs

    Diagnoses and fixes wiring, switches, and other electrical components to ensure safe operation.

  • Battery Service

    Tests, charges, or replaces batteries to guarantee reliable starts.

  • Pulley Replacement

    Replaces worn pulleys to maintain proper drive system function.

  • Spindle Service

    Repairs or replaces blade spindles for smooth, balanced blade rotation.

  • Deck Leveling

    Adjusts mower deck so blades sit at correct height and angle across entire cutting surface.
